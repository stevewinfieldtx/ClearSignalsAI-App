Four Intelligence Layers

Layer 1 — Cultural Foundation

8 cultural dimensions with dual professional and personal interpretations. Anchored by Hofstede scores. Three-tier inheritance: East/West → Regional → Country.

Layer 2 — Contextual Intelligence

Digital communication norms (email tone, preferred channels, response expectations, marketing copy style) and offline behavioral intelligence (greetings, meetings, negotiations, dining).

Layer 3 — Relationship Intelligence

Per-contact behavioral baselines: response time, formality score, warmth score, initiation ratio — each with culturally calibrated thresholds. Drift is measured against baseline, not a generic average.

Layer 4 — Scoring Rules

Machine-readable rules stored in vector DB for sub-500ms retrieval. Three rule sets per country: email scoring, video behavioral, and relationship drift — applied against Layer 3 baselines.
